What We Do

Our Core Services

We offer end-to-end wholesale and export solutions, from product sourcing to international delivery — handling every step with precision and professionalism.

Wholesale Distribution

We source and distribute a diverse portfolio of products — consumer goods, electronics, apparel, food products, industrial supplies, and more — at competitive wholesale prices for bulk buyers.

International Export

Specializing in export logistics to Latin America, we handle documentation, compliance, and coordination to ensure your shipment arrives on time and in full regulatory compliance.

Supplier Partnerships

We are actively seeking reliable suppliers and manufacturers across all product categories. If you produce quality goods and are looking for a U.S.-based distribution partner, we want to hear from you.

Logistics & Freight

We coordinate freight forwarding, customs brokerage, and last-mile delivery, offering seamless end-to-end logistics for both container and LCL shipments to Latin American destinations.

Trade Finance Support

We operate with full financial transparency and documentation standards designed to meet the requirements of banking institutions, trade credit applications, and international payment processors.

Procurement & Sourcing

From market research to vendor qualification and purchase order management, we provide professional procurement services to help clients secure the best products at the most favorable terms.
